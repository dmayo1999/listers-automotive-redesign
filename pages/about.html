The Owner

Butch Lister

When Butch Lister opened the doors at 1730 Main Street NE in 2001, the plan was simple: do honest work, charge a fair price, and stand behind every job. Twenty-five years later, that plan hasn't changed.

Butch built Lister's Automotive on a commitment to transparency that the auto repair industry doesn't always offer. Before we touch your car, you'll know what we found and what it'll cost. If there's a less expensive way to solve a problem — like sealing a transmission leak instead of ordering new parts — we'll tell you.

Jessica and the rest of the team carry the same values. You'll get a call when your car is ready, not a surprise on the invoice.

"You can always ask us about options to keep your auto repair cost low."

— Butch Lister, Owner
